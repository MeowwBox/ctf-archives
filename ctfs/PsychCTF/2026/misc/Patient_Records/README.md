The Ashworth Psychiatric Institute recently digitized their patient management system. The aging terminal in the intake ward lets staff admit patients, record their diagnoses, and discharge them when treatment is "complete."

The programmer who built it was... a former patient himself. They say he was obsessed with efficiency. Never wasted a cycle. Never cleaned up after himself.

Rumor has it he left something behind. Something that shouldn't still be there after a patient leaves.

The system keeps seeing ghosts of deleted patients. Maybe you can too.
