Milachu

I was in Times Square during the NYC New Year’s Eve ball drop and recorded the moment the countdown hit zero.

While reviewing the footage later, I noticed something unusual; a clean, isolated 1-second tone that shouldn’t exist in a live outdoor recording.

It’s not part of the official broadcast.
It’s not from the crowd.
And it’s far too deliberate to be accidental.

Something was hidden in the noise.

Can you uncover what really happened?
