We intercepted a mysterious FPGA bitstream and its encrypted output. The device was connected to a PS/2 keyboard - someone typed a secret message, the FPGA encrypted it, and the ciphertext was captured from the LED outputs.  The bitstream targets a Lattice iCE40 HX8K FPGA (CT256 package). You are given:  - firmware.bin - the iCE40 bitstream - ciphertext.hex - the captured ciphertext (hex-encoded, one 64-bit block per line) - pinout.md - pin assignment documentation
