Night Owls: Students who pre-record their clicker signals at home, then replay them late in class using a different device.

Legitimate students: respond in 2-15 seconds - normal human reading and thinking time.

During yesterday's lecture (March 29, 2026 at 2:30 PM), Professor Falcon's base station captured 20 seconds of RF traffic and logged every received packet with metadata. The legitimate students have hidden a message in their responses.

Your mission: separate cheaters from legitimate students.
