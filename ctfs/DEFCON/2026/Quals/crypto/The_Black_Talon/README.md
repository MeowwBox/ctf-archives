Egyptian Vulture
Neophron Percnopterus

Documented by f0xtr0t.

They call me The Vulture. A name earned through sins long forgotten, carried
alone in the silence that follows the death of those who knew. For decades,
I've lived quietly, thinking my glory days were buried with the fools who once
knew me. But whispers reach me of a town crawling with liars and thieves, and
a bar called the Black Talon. And a fortune rightfully mine. I will take it.
Let those poor sods drink and laugh. For now. By the time I'm done with this
town, even its dust shall fear me.
