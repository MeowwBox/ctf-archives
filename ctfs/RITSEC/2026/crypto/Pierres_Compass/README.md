There once was a French pirate named Pierre who built an enchanted compass. Each turn of its needle is driven by not one, but three hidden mechanisms (linear congruential generators) working together to chart his course.

It is said that Pierre's final message was lost to the seas... until now, of course.

We've managed to recover some of the inner workings (seeds and moduli) of the hidden mechanisms, but not all of them. We also found an alphabet and we believe Pierre used the outputs from his compass to index into the alphabet and send secret messages. Can you find the message he was trying to send?
