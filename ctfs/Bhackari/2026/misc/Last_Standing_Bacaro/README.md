🇬🇧: Many years ago, in the heart of Venessia, between the canai and the ancient maseghe stones, there was a bacaro that never closed its doors: the oldest, the most verace, the hardest to kill.

This bacaro was not like the others. Under the counter where they served folpo (octopus) and sarde in saor, the old owner kept a box of secrets: ancient recipes, code words, and the names of those who drank their ombri (wine) without paying. Everything was protected.

But as the years passed, things changed. Modern bars started popping up everywhere: spritz with Campari, fried chicken cicchetti, trap music, and Wi-Fi... The old oste had an idea: "I won't give up, I will protect my legacy!"

He took a photo of his beloved tavern and hid the secret inside, using a technique even the NSA might struggle with: the message is invisible to the eye, hidden deep within the colors...

Your Mission: Look closely at the photo. It is the breath of authentic Venice. If you want to extract the secret, you must think like a local and remember: the seed (passphrase) needed for this magic... is simply the name of the place.
