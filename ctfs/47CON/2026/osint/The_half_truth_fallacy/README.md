A group of intelligence officers is planning a behavioural intervention operation. We have discovered that they intend to distribute propaganda in order to shape the socio-economic context in favour of certain ideals. To this end, they will use a specific social media platform. In doing so, they will employ the well-known ‘half-truth fallacy’ as a method and strategy of social manipulation, in which something plausible is presented, and this is taken as a good reason to believe that the rest of the information is true. Our mission will be to put on our detective hats and uncover the truth behind these lies. It seems that the SUGUS website is hiding confidential information somewhere about this whole scheme. Will you be able to solve the case?

Note: a maximum number of 8 attempts per user to solve the challenge is set for this challenge, in order to avoid brute-force guessing.
