In 1974, a Cold War defense network went dark. Radar dishes stopped spinning and launch rails went cold. But not everything was erased. One site on the Jersey coast still stands, its missiles frozen in place like monuments to a war that never came. A signal was intercepted containing only these coordinates and a fragmented transmission: 40.4628, -73.9932 ...GUARDIAN... MEMORIAL... SITE DESIGNATION... CONFIRM... Find the site. Identify its military designation.
