English
An old IoT device was found while cleaning a study room. It appears to have been modified years ago to allow SSH login. After powering it on, the device still boots, and an SSH connection can still be established. However, the device is almost 20 years old. What was once considered secure may no longer be secure today. A packet capture of an SSH session has been provided. Recover the secret hidden in the session.

日本語
