Gorbino's quest of life
Knowledge is a powerful thing, we all know it. But what is the most powerful piece of knowledge one can take on a journey through life? Well, this is a trivial question - it's obviously 42. But what about the second most powerful? That's an interesting challenge.

That's the task you were given when approached by a wild gorbino. It intends to take your whisdom through a journey of life and hopes to finish its PhD in electrical engineering by the end of it. Unfortunately, while our computers are getting faster and faster, it still takes some time to simulate the couple years it takes to finish a diploma, so you better start thinking about whisdom instead of reading overly complicated task descriptions youngling.
