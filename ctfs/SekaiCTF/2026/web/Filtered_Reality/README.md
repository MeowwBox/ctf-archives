Description
The Ministry of Records calls The Archive the best-run bureau in the city: every citizen filing processed, every record reviewed, every day exactly like the last. You submit a record, the Archivist reviews it, and the day resets — clean, quiet, eternal. But somewhere in that endless cycle a clerk filed something they were never meant to, and the loop has been quietly re-filing it ever since. Slip a record past the Archivist, find the seam where the perfect day was stitched shut, and read what the Archive refuses to let go of.
